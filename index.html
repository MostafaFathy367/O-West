About Us

Welcome to O West, Orascom Development’s latest addition to its towns portfolio nestled in 6th of October. O West is set to rise as a beacon of integrated living. Envisioned as a complete sensory experience full of energy, playing differently to everyone, from shops to restaurants, sports club to common spaces, this place offers an experience you can taste, touch, smell, hear, see and more importantly; feel. It is constantly evolving and changing, adapting and growing. Like an Eco system, everything within O West is intelligently & harmoniously designed to offer a true wholesome town experience.

Location

Nestled in the heart of 6th of October, O West is easily accessible due to its proximity to main roads and highways. With its strategic position, O West is:

  • In close proximity to Wahat Road
  • 3 minutes from Mall of Egypt
  • 5 minutes from Dahshour Road
  • 7 minutes from Juhayna Square
  • 10 minutes from Ring Road
  • 12 minutes from Mehwar

neighborhoods

whyt

Reminiscent of our ancestors’ estates, Whyt villas, twin houses and town houses are set against a backdrop of greenery and open spaces. With all the components of true town living just steps away, residents’ lives come to a full circle at O West.

TULWA

From the ancient Egyptian meaning “to rise”, Tulwa lives up to the name with residences rising beyond regular expectations, delivering highly efficient and functional living spaces. Homeowners get to experience true town living with apartments, duplexes, lofts, & penthouses steps away from everything they need for a balanced.

O BUSINESS DISTRICT

O Business District is set to be Egypt’s most prominent commercial village, a dynamic zone with out-of-the-ordinary office areas for creative business owners.

FERA RESIDENCES

O West’s Fera presents a community of an unparalleled lifestyle, a community of elegance and elite luxuriant units with equally mesmerizing views.
